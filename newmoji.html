Newmojis, a new kind of emojis for oddly specific moments

  • Sarcastic Laughter

    For the dry stiffled laugther you give to your friends when they tell a dad joke. This Newmoji is the epitome of "haha that was so funny" texts.

  • Why Is It Spicy?

    Have you ever tried McDonald's Sprite? Or bitten into a Ricola cough drop? That feeling of surprise and intense spice is pictured with this Newmoji.

  • Awkward Wave

    That awkward moment when you wave to an acquantance and they don't see you (or even worse, don't recognize you).

  • In My Feels

    For when you're vibing to your music and caught up in the moment of the tunes.

  • "Frozen" on Zoom

    For the "sorry, my wifi cut out" moments. In reality, you just weren't prepared to answer the cold call question your supervisor asked you.
