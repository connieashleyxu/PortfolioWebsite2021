Unpacking the Network Experience

  • I'm one to ask for help.

    Those who are willing to be open with others often find it easier to create meaningful connections with others.

    In the case of the Network Experience, you may have noticed that new networks were created with each click signifying that those who are willing to be vulnerable create long-lasting networks easier.

  • I'm NOT one to ask for help.

    Those who are not as open with others tend to find it more difficult to create meaningful connections with others.

    In the case of the Network Experience, you may have noticed networks being created but instantly vanishing. This phenomenon signifies the lack of a strong connection between a group because of a lack of personable interactions.
